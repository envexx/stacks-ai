### 3. Vision - Problem Statement

**Recommended Text (150-200 words):**

```
Traditional AI API services force users into expensive monthly subscriptions 
($20-200/month) regardless of actual usage, creating barriers for:

1. Casual users who only need AI occasionally
2. Developers in emerging markets without credit cards
3. Projects requiring transparent, verifiable payment trails
4. Users seeking privacy without centralized payment processors

Additionally, existing payment systems lack:
- Transparency: Hidden fees and unclear pricing
- Accessibility: 1.7 billion unbanked people excluded
- Verification: No on-chain proof of payment
- Fairness: Pay for unused capacity

StacksAI solves this by implementing the x402-stacks protocol, enabling 
blockchain-verified micropayments for AI access. Users pay only for what 
they use (as low as $0.04 per prompt) using STX tokens, with every 
transaction verified on the Stacks blockchain and secured by Bitcoin.

This creates a fairer, more accessible AI ecosystem while demonstrating 
real-world utility for the Stacks blockchain beyond DeFi applications.
```
